WASIM MALIK

PHOTOGRAPHY

I'm Wasim Malik, a Ladakh based Photographer and Filmmaker. I capture nature’s beauty, from vast landscapes to intimate portraits, telling stories with every frame.

Services

Landscape Photography

Capturing the breathtaking beauty of nature, from serene mountains to vast open fileds. Every shot tells a story of the worlds most stunning landscapes

Travel Photography

Exploring new places and documenting unforgettable moments through the lens. Whether it’s hidden gems or famous destinations, we bring them to life.

Portrait Photography

Creating timeless portraits with natural lighting and beautiful compositions. Perfect for personal, professional, or creative shoots.
